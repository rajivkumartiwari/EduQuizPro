About EduQuiz Pro

A student-centred digital learning platform built by teachers, for students ” making computer science education engaging, structured, and accessible from Class 6 to Class 12.

🎯

Our Mission

Our mission is to deliver interactive, curriculum-aligned computer science education through quizzes, notes, and resources ” empowering every student to learn at their own pace and excel in board exams, practicals, and viva voce sessions.

We believe that learning happens best when it is active, not passive. Every quiz on this platform is designed not just to test, but to teach ” with instant feedback, score reviews, and structured topic progression.

  • ✓ Curriculum aligned with CBSE & ICSE syllabi
  • ✓ Instant feedback on every answer
  • ✓ Topic-wise progression from basics to advanced
  • ✓ Viva and practical exam preparation built in
🔭

Our Vision

To become the go-to digital companion for every computer science student ” a platform where learning feels like play, every concept is a challenge worth solving, and progress is always celebrated.

We envision a future where every classroom in India has access to high-quality, freely available digital learning tools that match the rigour of modern computer science education ” and that every student, regardless of background, can walk into an exam with full confidence.

School Policies

Our classroom is built on three pillars: Integrity, Curiosity, and Respect. These values guide how we teach and how we expect students to engage with this platform.

▶
Academic Honesty

All quizzes are for practice and formative self-assessment only. Students are expected to attempt them independently and use the feedback to genuinely improve ” not to copy or share answers.

▶
Respectful Engagement

Students and teachers engage with this platform with mutual respect. Any misuse, tampering with quiz logic, or accessing admin areas without permission is strictly prohibited.

▶
Data & Privacy

No personal data is collected or stored on any external server. All quiz scores are saved locally in your browser and are visible only to you.

▶
Device Usage

This platform is designed to be used on school computers and personal devices alike. Students are responsible for appropriate device use during school hours as per the school's IT usage policy.
